--- title: About ---

I like to write, I think that is what describes me the best. Whether it is writing poetry, short stories, essays, a mathematical proof or coding a bit, it does not really matter to me. My published "software" projects can be found on GitHub. The point of this site, on the other hand, is to share some of my readable ideas with the world, although I have no expectations anyone will ever read them.

Right now I am not focused on anything particular besides finishing studies at FI MUNI. When it comes to computer science, I am mostly interested in automata theory. My three programming languages of choice would be Haskell, Javascript and C++ (in order). But overall, I am interested in far too many things, sometimes I wish it was the contrary.

If you had been intrigued by what I do, an unlikely event happened and I would be more than glad to hear your opinion! Just drop me an e-mail or tweet me @fiedlr.
